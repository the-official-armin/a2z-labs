# A2Z Labs Roadmap

> **Mission:** Learn Artificial Intelligence from **A to Z**

---

# Vision

A2Z Labs is a long-term personal AI research and engineering laboratory.

This repository is not intended to be a collection of tutorials or course assignments.

Its purpose is to document the complete learning journey through:

* Research Papers
* Projects
* Experiments
* Engineering Challenges
* Production Systems
* Weekly Research Journals

The goal is to understand **how AI works**, **why it works**, and **how to build it**.

---

# Lab Philosophy

## Build

Every concept should become a project.

## Experiment

Every project should answer a question.

## Understand

Focus on understanding over memorization.

## Document

Record both successes and failures.

## Improve

Projects evolve. Nothing is ever truly "finished."
